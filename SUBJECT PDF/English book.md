 🧒 UNIT 1: Me and My Body

 ✨ Poem: My Little Body

```
I have two hands to clap-clap-clap,
Two little feet to tap-tap-tap.
I see with eyes, I hear with ears,
My little body I love so dear!
```

 🖍 New Words

hand, leg, eye, ear, nose, mouth

 🎤 Let Us Speak

• I clap with my hands
• I walk with my legs

 ✏ Activity

👉 Draw your face and colour it
👉 Circle: eye 👁 ear 👂 nose 👃

---

 👨‍👩‍👧 UNIT 2: My Family

 📖 Story: My Sweet Family

```
This is my family.
My mother loves me.
My father helps me.
We eat and play together.
I love my family.
```

 🖍 New Words

mother, father, brother, sister, family

 🎨 Activity

👉 Draw your family
👉 Say: “I love my _____”

---

 🐶 UNIT 3: Animals Around Me

 📘 My Animal Friends

```
Dog says bow-wow  
Cat says meow  
Cow gives us milk  
I love animals all!
```

 🖍 New Words

dog, cat, cow, bird, fish

 🎯 Activity

👉 Match animal to sound
👉 Colour your favourite animal

---

 🍎 UNIT 4: Food We Eat

 🍓 Poem: Yummy Food

```
Apple red and banana yellow,
Milk is white and mango mellow.
Food gives strength, food gives cheer,
Eat your food, my dear!
```

 🥕 New Words

apple, milk, rice, fruit, vegetable

 🧠 Talk Time

• What do you eat at home?
• What is your favourite food?

 ☀️ UNIT 5: Seasons and Weather

 📚 UNIT 8: Rhymes & Short Stories

 🐭 Story: The Clever Mouse

```

```
